The Basics...

There 5 things you will be using on your vehicle. Those 5 things are:
the throttle, the clutch, the shifter,the rear brake and the front brake.
The throttle is what you use to accelerate and is attached to your right grip,
The clutch is activated by the left lever and is what you use to go through gears
and can be used for raising your front tire, the front brake is on your right lever
but shouldn't be used a lot as your primarily using your rear brake.
