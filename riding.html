The Basics...

There 5 things you will be using on your vehicle. Those 5 things
are: throttle , clutch , shifter , rear brake and the front brake
The throttle is what you use to accelerate and is attached to your right grip.
The clutch is activated by the left lever and is needed to shift gears
and can be used for more advanced tricks or tactics. The shifter should
be on the left side by your toe and is used to shift, you push down
to shift into first gear and the rest are usually upwards (the video
explains more in depth). The front brake is connected to your right
lever but shouldn't be used a lot as your primarily using your rear brake.
